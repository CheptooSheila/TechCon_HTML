History

TechCon started in 2010 with the goal of bringing together thought leaders, technologists, and enthusiasts from around the world to discuss the latest advancements in technology. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing in the technology industry. We believe in empowering individuals and organizations to drive the future of tech through meaningful discussions and hands-on experiences.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in technology. Here are a few notable speakers:

Elon Musk - CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, gave a keynote speech in 2015 on the future of space exploration and sustainable energy.

Sheryl Sandberg - COO of Facebook, shared insights on leadership, technology, and gender equality in tech at the 2018 event.

Sundar Pichai - CEO of Google, discussed the role of AI and machine learning in shaping the future of digital experiences at TechCon 2020.
